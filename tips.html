Travel Tips

"Guidelines for a smooth and safe exploration journey"

Public buses and minivans connect major towns like Tacurong City and Isulan. For remote locations, hiring a local tricycle or riding a specialized "habal-habal" motorcycle is your primary option.

Sultan Kudarat has a prominent Muslim and Indigenous community. Dress modestly when visiting community landmarks or religious sites, and always ask permission before taking photos of locals.

While urban hubs like Tacurong have operational ATMs, eco-destinations and remote beach resorts transact strictly in cash. Bring enough Philippine Pesos (PHP) along.

The dry summer months (December to May) are ideal for coastal beaches, caving, and clear mountain trekking.

Practice the "Leave No Trace" principle. Dispose of your trash correctly and support local economies by purchasing authentic regional items.
