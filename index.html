Welcome to Sultan Kudarat

Where rich cultural heritage meets untouched natural beauty! Named after one of our country's greatest and bravest historical Muslim rulers, our province is a proud home to friendly communities, misty mountain ridges, mysterious caves, and beautiful wildlife sanctuaries. If you are looking for an authentic adventure filled with warm smiles and hidden treasures, you have found the perfect destination. Your unforgettable Mindanao journey starts here!

About the Province

Get to know the golden soul of Mindanao

Welcome to the Golden Province

A Beautiful Blend of Culture & Adventure

Officially established in 1973 and named after the legendary Muslim ruler Sultan Muhammad Dipatuan Kudarat, our province is a peaceful melting pot where Christian settlers, Muslim tribes, and indigenous Lumad groups live in rich harmony. From high mountain ridges to massive underground river systems, a unique journey awaits you.
