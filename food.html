A Taste of Sultan Kudarat

Discover the authentic flavors and rich culinary traditions of the region

Pastil

Steamed rice topped with shredded spiced chicken, fish, or beef flakes wrapped tightly in fresh banana leaves.

Where: Local markets & street stalls across Tacurong City

Tinagtag

A crispy traditional sweet treat made from ground rice flour and sugar syrup fried into fine, mesh-like brown layers.

Where: Cultural festivals & traditional Maguindanaon homes

Beef Kulma

A rich, aromatic beef stew curry made creamy with thick coconut milk, peanuts, and flavorful traditional spices.

Where: Authentic halal restaurants in Isulan & Palimbang

Fine Robusta Coffee

Premium local coffee blends harvested carefully from highland organic coffee fields across Lebak and Senator Ninoy Aquino.

Where: Highland cafes and souvenir shops
